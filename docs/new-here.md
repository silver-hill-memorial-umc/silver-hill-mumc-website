# New Here? Welcome!

We're so glad you're considering visiting Silver Hill Memorial United Methodist Church! Whether you're new to the area, exploring your faith, or looking for a church home, we want you to feel welcomed and at ease.

## What to Expect

### Service Time & Location

**Sunday Morning Worship:** 10:00 AM Eastern Time

**Address:** 778 John B White Sr Blvd, Spartanburg, SC 29306

[Get Directions on Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/eJUeRBVKxBakPmEJ7){ .md-button .md-button--primary }

### When You Arrive

- **Parking:** Free parking is available in our church lot
- **Greeting:** You'll be warmly welcomed at the door by our greeters
- **Seating:** Sit wherever you're comfortable - there's no assigned seating
- **Duration:** Our service typically lasts about one hour

### What to Wear

Come as you are! You'll see a mix of styles at Silver Hill - from suits and dresses to jeans and casual wear. We care more about your presence than your wardrobe.

### What Happens During Service

Our Sunday worship service includes:

- **Prelude** - A time of preparation and reflection
- **Call to Worship** - Coming together as a community of faith
- **Hymns & Music** - Traditional and contemporary songs of praise
- **Prayer** - Confession, intercession, and thanksgiving
- **Scripture Reading** - Hearing God's word proclaimed
- **Sermon** - Biblical teaching that connects to daily life
- **Response** - Altar call and commitment time
- **Benediction** - Sending forth with blessing

### Children & Families

Children are always welcome in our worship service! We believe the sounds of children are a blessing to our community.

- **Children's resources** are available to help little ones engage during service
- **Nursery care** may be available - please ask a greeter for details

## Accessibility

Silver Hill Memorial UMC is committed to being accessible to all:

- **Wheelchair accessible** facility
- **Large print** materials available upon request
- **Assistive listening devices** available - ask an usher

## About Our Church

Silver Hill Memorial UMC is one of the oldest churches in the Spartanburg area, with a rich history of faithful service to our community. We are part of the United Methodist Church and embrace the Wesleyan tradition of:

- **Open Hearts** - God's love is for everyone
- **Open Minds** - We think critically about our faith
- **Open Doors** - All are welcome here
