## How Your Gifts Make a Difference

Your contributions support:
- **Worship Services** - Music, technology, and worship materials
- **Community Outreach** - Local mission and service projects
- **Building Maintenance** - Keeping our sanctuary and facilities welcoming
- **Pastoral Care** - Supporting our pastoral ministry and care programs
